# DEVELOPMENT PLAN

## Phase 1: Research and Planning  
**Timeline:** Jan 2026 - Feb 2026  
**Milestones:**  
- Complete market research  
- Identify key features  
- Define project scope  
**Tasks:**  
1. Conduct competitor analysis  
2. Gather user requirements  
3. Draft project scope document  
**Dependencies:**  
- Market research


## Phase 2: Design  
**Timeline:** Mar 2026 - Apr 2026  
**Milestones:**  
- Finalize UI/UX design  
- Create wireframes and prototypes  
**Tasks:**  
1. Design mockups using Figma  
2. Conduct design reviews  
3. Finalize prototype  
**Dependencies:**  
- Completion of Phase 1


## Phase 3: Development  
**Timeline:** May 2026 - Aug 2026  
**Milestones:**  
- Complete initial development  
- Achieve alpha release  
**Tasks:**  
1. Develop core features  
2. Set up CI/CD pipelines  
3. Conduct unit testing  
**Dependencies:**  
- Completion of Phase 2


## Phase 4: Testing  
**Timeline:** Sep 2026 - Oct 2026  
**Milestones:**  
- Complete testing phase  
- Achieve beta release  
**Tasks:**  
1. Perform system testing  
2. Conduct user acceptance testing  
3. Gather feedback and make improvements  
**Dependencies:**  
- Completion of Phase 3


## Phase 5: Launch and Post-Launch  
**Timeline:** Nov 2026  
**Milestones:**  
- Launch product to market  
- Gather user feedback  
- Implement updates based on feedback  
**Tasks:**  
1. Create launch plan  
2. Monitor product performance  
3. Gather user analytics  
**Dependencies:**  
- Completion of Phase 4


## Sprint Planning  
- Each phase will be divided into sprints of 2 weeks.  
- Sprint reviews will be conducted at the end of each sprint to assess progress and adjust plans accordingly.

## Conclusion  
This development plan outlines the structured approach to building the travel AI assistant. Each phase is designed to ensure thorough preparation, execution, and post-launch evaluation to achieve a successful product launch.
